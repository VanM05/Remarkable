THE THING I LIKE THE MOST WHEN I'M IN PORTUGAL

I spent my entire childhood running around beaches, swimming, climbing trees, and basically being in constant contact with nature due to the area I live in. But Portugal is not only nature, it also has amazing urban areas with incredible architecture that are fantastic to visit and live. What I like the most about Portugal is that the food is always fresh, and that we have an amazing selectivity of fresh fish to choose from. I like visiting Lisbon, the capital, and enjoying the shows and old streets full of history the city has to offer.

WHAT I MISS THE MOST WHEN I'M AWAY IS...

This might sound silly but one of the things that I miss the most when I am in the UK is the bread. Portuguese bread is amazingly fresh, and everyone has the habit to buy freshly baked bread and pastries. While living in Nottingham, I also realised how much I missed the beach that back home is literally a 3-minute walk away.
