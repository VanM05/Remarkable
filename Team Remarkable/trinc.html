THE THING I LIKE THE MOST WHEN I'M IN NIGERIA

The wildlife and the colourful festivals that we have all year round. Nigeria is a huge country with vast farmlands, I like helping in the community during harvest season. I also like the museums and theme parks, they are great for family outings.

WHAT I MISS THE MOST WHEN I'M AWAY IS...

The food!! Nigeria is home to 300 different tribes all with their own food, languages, and traditions so there is always a chance to try different food. I also miss the beaches and rainforests.

FUN FACTS!

    • Nigeria has a huge population of 201 million people.

    • In Nigeria, its seen as disrespectful to eat or give something to somebody with your left hand.

    • Nigeria is home to the richest man in Africa, Aliko Dangote.
